# RoadWatch AI Microservice Platform

This repository contains the AI Microservice Platform for RoadWatch — a scalable, Kubernetes-native AI orchestration layer.

Components:
- FastAPI AI Orchestrator (coordinates AI services)
- Computer Vision service (road damage detection)
- NLP service (complaint understanding)
- Geo Intelligence service (road ownership)
- OCR service (document extraction)
- RAG service (road knowledge retrieval)
- Analytics service (governance insights)

See `docs/architecture.md` for design details and usage.
