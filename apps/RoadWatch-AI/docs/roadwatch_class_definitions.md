RoadWatch Class Definitions

1) pothole
- Description: Depressions or holes in the road surface caused by material loss.
- Visual cues: dark irregular cavity, shadowing, disrupted pavement texture.

2) crack
- Description: Linear fissures in pavement; can be hairline to deep.
- Visual cues: thin dark lines often following road direction; may branch.

3) waterlogging
- Description: Accumulated standing water on road surface.
- Visual cues: blueish/reflective patches, surface sheen, reflections.

4) surface wear
- Description: Worn pavement, skid marks, raveling, exposed aggregate.
- Visual cues: faded texture, color changes, roughness patches.

5) missing road marking
- Description: Absence or heavy fading of lane markings, crosswalks, or guidance lines.
- Visual cues: long gaps where markings expected; faded or discontinuous paint.

6) broken signboard
- Description: Street signs that are bent, missing panels, or significantly damaged.
- Visual cues: missing panels, rotated/bent signs, broken supports.
